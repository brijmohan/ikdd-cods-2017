Intermediate results for 70 test words.

eng.dict	- English Grapheme to Phoneme (G2P) output of 70 words
hin.dict	- Hindi Grapheme to Phoneme (G2P) output of 70 words
ranks.txt	- Rank of each word based on own metric
rhyme.txt	- Rhymes of each word from a monolingual Hindi corpus
scores.txt	- Scores based on 6 different complementary systems
syll.txt	- Syllables of each word based on Hindi lexicon
trans.txt	- Devanagari transliteration of each English word
